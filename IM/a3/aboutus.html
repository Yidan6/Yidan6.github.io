Personal Reflection

This coffee website has a central idea of providing users with a playful experience and giving them feedback when they click or browse. Firstly, there is a homepage with two buttons in the center: "Shop" and "About Us". Clicking on "Shop" takes the user to the shopping page, where three types of products are presented: coffee beans, gift boxes, and ceramic coffee cups. They are displayed as images, and when the mouse is hovered over each image, a tooltip pops up with two buttons: "Buy Now" and "Add to Cart". Since the "Buy Now" button would typically lead to a checkout page for customers to provide their information and make payment, you haven't designed a specific page for it.

There is only one hover effect, where the image changes color when the mouse is placed over it. When the "Add to Cart" button is clicked, the selected product is added to the cart, which is located in the top right corner of the page. The cart icon displays a number indicating the quantity of items added. Initially, you also planned to add a hover effect to the cart, where the previously added items would be displayed in the form of a list when the mouse is placed over it. However, due to technical challenges, such as the information being blocked by the product images, you were unable to implement this feature.

Additionally, you have added background music to the website and sound effects to the buttons. This provides users with feedback as the website responds differently to their interactions. Initially, you had an idea to create a web-based game where users could interact with the website to create their own cup of coffee. However, due to the complexity of using software like Unity for modeling and implementation, you decided to focus primarily on the central interaction of adding products to the cart. As for the animation effect, you have implemented a feature where the products appear to jump into the cart. Regarding the "About Us" page, clicking on it leads to the current personal reflection section.

Here are the key points for each page and functionality:

Homepage layout:

The homepage features a clean design with the main content centered.

Two prominent buttons, "Shop" and "About Us," are placed in the middle, providing direct access to the main functionalities.

The main title, "Welcome to Coffee Website," informs users about the purpose of the website, accompanied by a short emotional message.

Shopping page:

The shopping page displays product photos.

When the mouse is hovered over a product image, a tooltip pops up, showing detailed information about the product.

Within the tooltip, users can find two buttons: "Buy Now" and "Add to Cart," enhancing the interaction between users and products.

Cart functionality:

When the "Add to Cart" button is clicked, the selected product is displayed next to the cart icon in the top right corner, with the cart icon indicating the number of items added. This provides real-time feedback, allowing users to see the quantity of items in their cart.

Hover effects and button sounds:

Product images change color when the mouse hovers over them, providing visual feedback.

Button sounds add interactivity; playing a sound effect when users click buttons enhances the feedback and entertainment value.

Background music:

Background music is added to create ambiance and enhance the entertainment value. This provides an additional sensory experience for users while browsing the website.
